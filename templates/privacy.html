Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information that you provide directly to us. This includes your account information (name, email address, password), and any financial data you choose to enter such as expenses, categories, and notes. We do not collect any data from third parties or access your bank accounts directly.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect solely to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Your data is used to display your expense history, generate summaries and insights about your spending patterns, and allow you to filter and analyze your financial transactions. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers with industry-standard encryption and security measures. We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties. We may use third-party service providers to host our application and database, but these providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and are not permitted to use it for any purpose other than providing services to us.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time through your account settings. You may also export all your data in a machine-readable format. If you choose to delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

6. Cookies and Analytics

We use minimal cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We do not use tracking cookies or share browsing information with advertisers. Our analytics are limited to aggregated, anonymized usage statistics to help us improve the application.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the application. We are committed to addressing any concerns you may have about your privacy.
